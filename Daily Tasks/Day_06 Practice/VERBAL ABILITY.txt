                                                                                           VERBAL ABILITY
Reading Passage

Databases are at the heart of modern applications. Efficient storage,
retrieval, and management of data are essential for building scalable
software products. Companies rely on well-designed databases to ensure
data consistency and performance.

Questions:

1. Why are databases important in modern applications?
Answer:
Because they enable efficient storage, retrieval, and management of data, 
which is essential for building scalable and reliable applications.

2. What does data consistency mean?
Answer:
Data consistency means that the data remains accurate, uniform, 
and reliable across the database at all times, even after updates.

3. Why do companies need well-designed databases?
Answer:
Companies need well-designed databases to ensure:
High performance
Data consistency
Scalability for large applications
